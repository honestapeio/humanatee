# Humanatee

**Verified human creation in an AI-first world.**

Humanatee is an open, developer-first system for attesting that digital content was created through meaningful human effort.

As AI-generated media becomes indistinguishable from human work, creators, platforms, and audiences lose trust. Humanatee restores provenance by providing cryptographic proof, transparent records, and public verification.

---

## Why Humanatee Exists

Digital culture is entering a provenance crisis.

- AI can generate text, images, audio, and video at scale.
- Platforms cannot reliably distinguish human work.
- Creators lose credit and economic leverage.
- Audiences lose trust.

Humanatee is built to solve this by making **human effort verifiable.**

We are not anti-AI.
We are pro-human.

---

## What Humanatee Does (v0.1)

Version 0.1 focuses on the minimum viable proof:

- Manual human attestation
- Content hashing
- Cryptographic signing
- Public verification records
- Creator profiles

No tokens. No staking. No speculation.

Just proof.

---

## How It Works (MVP)

1. A creator submits content
2. Humanatee generates a cryptographic hash
3. The creator signs an attestation
4. A public record is created
5. Anyone can verify the proof

This creates a permanent link between a human and their work.
